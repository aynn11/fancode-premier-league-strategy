# FanCode x Premier League — Corporate Strategy

> Self-initiated strategy project analysing FanCode's market position 
> and building a data-backed GTM recommendation for the Founders' Office.


## The Problem I Identified

FanCode has a brand invisibility problem. In a market where JioCinema 
paid ₹23,758 Cr for IPL rights and Star Sports owns cricket's legacy 
audience, FanCode has no anchor content — and no clear reason for a 
casual sports fan to open the app first.

This project answers: what should FanCode do about it, and why?


## What's Inside

- **Gap analysis** of FanCode's current market position vs Hotstar, 
  JioCinema, and Star Sports
- **Target audience personas** — 2 underserved segments with 
  acquisition logic
- **GTM strategy** across 3 phases with explicit trade-offs
- **Revenue model** projecting economics across audience profiles
- **Premier League rights case** — why EPL 2027 is the right bet, 
  what it costs, and what it unlocks
- **Risk analysis** and mitigation framework


## Output

10-slide consulting-grade strategy deck built for senior leadership 
consumption — structured as problem → market → options → recommendation 
→ revenue logic.
